High Growth · DPS Check
Cooldown chain
Growth will cause tanks to take increasing damage until Gruul begins to one shot the raid. Use defensive cooldowns as Growth stacks climb high. Hurtful Strike tank must not drop second threat.
Massive Healing
Growth will cause tanks to take increasing damage until Gruul begins to one shot the raid. As stacks build high use major healing cooldowns and mana potions. This is the burn window, do not conserve mana for much longer.
Execute kill window
Growth will with enough stacks eventually cause the tanks to take unhealable fatal blows. This is not preventable and is a soft-enrage mechanic built into the fight. Gruul either dies here or you do.
