Pull on Mage Tank · Synchronized Misdirect Follows · Lock All Four Adds in Place · Never Split DPS off Maulgar
Pre-position before pull
All 4 tanks at their assigned spots BEFORE pull. Kiggler tank in upper-left corner. Mage tank in right corner. They DO NOT move from these spots the entire fight.
Mage Pulls and the raid follows
Hunter MDs Maulgar onto Main tank. Hunter MDs Olm onto Off tank while he picks ups Blindeye. Both shoot together. Kiggler and Krosh are tanked at range and kept still the entire encounter.
Maulgar against the wall
Main tank faces Maulgar AWAY from raid (Arcing Smash cleaves), back to south west wall (Mighty Blow knockback negated).
Don't heal during MD shot
Threat from heals during the first 3-4 seconds will overwrite MD. Wait for tanks to lock aggro before any big healing.
Each healer locks on their tank
Healers follow assignments and help other healers if you can get away with it. Minimize healing during initial threat. Tanks need 5s to lock aggro before heals come in.
DPS waits ~5s after pull
Tanks need a moment to lock threat. Hard stop until Main tank calls "go."
Warlock enslaves Olm's felhunters
Olm summons Wild Fel Stalkers throughout the fight. Enslave one to use it's taunt and other ability on Olm; OT cleaves any new ones until the warlock can enslave.
This is a Maulgar first and only strategy
Adds will not die. They continue to fight when Maulgar dies. DO NOT split DPS onto and add except cleaving blindeye's shield off. Every GCD on an add aside from blindeye management is wasted.
Phase 1 · Maulgar 100% → 50% · Maintain Kicks on Blindeye and Enslaves on Demons · Four Adds Must Be Controlled
Whirlwind · Tank Stays in
Maulgar Whirlwinds periodically. Main Tank remains on the boss.
Mage tank spellsteals Spell Shield
Mage tank spellsteals Krosh's Spell Shield for fire damage absorb. Stay in corner.
Kiggler Hex on tank
Kiggler hexes the highest-threat target periodically (drops aggro). Kiggler tank is moonkin (immune) OR 2 hunters rotating threat.
Off tank holds Blindeye + Olm
Off tank stacks both adds on the same spot. Death Coil + Olm's felhunter spawns hit raid intermittently. OT cleaves felhunters as they pop.
Healer cooldowns NOW
Save nothing for "later" — this IS later. Innervates, mana pots, racials. Race the kill.
Stay 15y+ from Krosh
15-yard fire AoE. Stay 15y+ from Krosh. Mage tank takes the hit; Mage tank healer stays in range to heal them but outside Blast Wave.
Death Coil on raid
Olm Death Coils periodically — heavy single-target damage. Raid healers watch raid frames closely.
Kiggler Lightning Bolts
Kiggler casts Lightning Bolts on highest-threat (his tank) and AoE Greater Polymorph: Frog. Polymorphed players are out of the fight 6s — healers spot them.
Bloodlust + cooldowns
Pop everything. Trinkets, racials, potions, BL. Maulgar 50→0 should take ~30 seconds — longer = increasing risk of fear/charge overlap kills.
Kicks on Blindeye Greater Heal
Kicker 1 and Kicker 2 stagger interrupts on Greater Heal. Power World: Shield blocks kicks so raid burns it off with cleave.
Stay 15y+ from Krosh
Blast Wave hits anyone within 15y. Position raid central or south — far from Krosh's right corner.
Run out on Whirlwind
Melee disengage when Maulgar Whirlwinds. Re-engage after — 25-35s safe window before next.
Phase 2 · Maulgar 50% → 0% · Flurry + Charge + Intimidating Roar · Healers Lock In
Flurry at 50%
Maulgar's damage spikes hard. Main tank rotates defensives: Shield Wall, Shield Block, trinkets.
Maulgar can charge into the raid
Maulgar charges, stuns, leaves MT. Off tank must move the boss back into position out of the raid to stop whirlwind from wiping the raid.
Intimidating Roar (fear)
Stuns MT, fears nearby raid for ~4s. Main tank stance-dances or trinkets out. Tremor Totem if available.
Other 3 tanks: hold the line
Kiggler, Krosh, Blindeye/Olm tanks keep doing what they were doing. Their adds will despawn when Maulgar dies.
MT damage doubles
Flurry phase. MT healer tunnel heals. Other healers chip in if their tank is stable.
Charge stuns the target
When Maulgar charges someone, they're stunned + take damage. Raid healers instant-cast on charge target.
EVERY DPS ON MAULGAR
No exceptions. Adds are tank-locked — do not assist on them. Burn Maulgar from 100 to 50 as fast as possible.
Kicks still mandatory
Blindeye's Greater Heal during Phase 2 = wipe. Kickers do not let up. Don't let dying-Maulgar get healed back to 60%.
Whirlwind + Charge overlap
If Maulgar Whirlwinds right after Charging into the raid, casters can die instantly. Spread out, don't cluster.
Maulgar Dies · Adds Continue Fighting · Everyone Runs OUT of the Instance
Adds keep fighting
When High King Maulgar dies, all four adds continue the encounter. Maintain threat and run them to the door as the raid escapes. If you are tanking Kiggler or Krosh hold them until as many of the raiders escape as possible. One you are outside raid lead will call when it is safe to enter again.
Run out the instance entrance
Whole raid runs back to the instance portal and exits. Keep healing tanks and raiders as they flee toward the portal. If the tanks die they will start to one shot everyone running out. Don't stop healing as you run out. Once you exit raid lead will call when it is safe to enter again.
Run out immediately
When High King Maulgar dies run out of the instance and disregard all of the adds. Sprint out as fast as possible. Raid lead will call when it is safe to enter again.
Pre-pull positions
All 4 tanks pre-positioned. Kiggler tank in upper-left (do not move). Mage tank in right corner (do not move). MT pulls Maulgar south against the wall. OT pulls Blindeye + Olm to the left side.
During the fight
Each tank/healer pair is locked into their position. Raid clusters south of Maulgar (out of Blast Wave from Krosh, away from Kiggler corner). Melee runs out on Whirlwind, returns when safe.
Wipe Causes
  • Hunter arcane shots Krosh and purges the same shield the mage tank needs to spellsteal to survive.
  • Split DPS to an add wasting valueable GCDs that should be on Maulgar.
  • Miss a kick on Blindeye's Greater Heal and he heals for 30%.
  • Raid fails to clear Blindeye's Power Word: Shield making him kick immune.
  • Standing in front of Maulgar's cleave.
  • Standing within 15y of Krosh will trigger a blast wave that can one shot.
  • Standing within 15y of Kiggler triggering arcane explosion.
  • Tanks and warlocks not picking up demons that then kill healers.
  • Melee stays in for whirlwind taking 7k+ on plate and instant death on cloth.
  • Maulgar charges the raid in P2, tanks do not relocate him and whirlwinds the raid to death.
  • Kiggler tank moves out of corner and Kiggler walks into raid, AoE damage everywhere.
  • Krosh tank moves out of corner and Krosh walks into raid, Blast Wave one shots most.
