Phase 1 · Split north + south · Interrupt casts · Banish and CC adds
Main Tank South
Main Tank holds Skull, X, and Moon south side. Do not go close to the tank up north to ensure the mobs' spells stay ranged from the raid.
Off Tank North
Off Tank holds Square and Diamond in place at the north wall. Do not bring them close to the raid or their spell casts will be in range.
North Healer
The healer for Off Tank should be close by and the only other player up north taking Shadow Bolt Volleys.
Assigned kicks
Interrupt your marked channeler and communicate with your partner in rotation. Be vocal. Good comms save raids.
Help CC Adds
The infernals can kill before a warlock gets Banish off. If you see one free, a quick CC can give warlocks time. Stuns, fears, and other CCs work, but do not get off kill target long.
Follow Kill Order
Adds should be killed with focused single-target damage and cleave, not AoE. Follow the kill order to keep raid damage and phase one duration to a minimum.
Phase 2 · Click assigned cube on Blast Nova
Mag Faces North
Main Tank keeps Magtheridon central in the room facing him away from the raid, looking north. Magtheridon's cleave will one-shot DPS.
Tank and Spank
Phase two of Magtheridon is simple to tank. Stand still with your back to the north wall and do not let Magtheridon turn on the raid.
Cover cube clickers
Top clickers. They will take damage when they channel. Stabilize the raid during cube activation.
Quake Disruption
Periodically Magtheridon will cast Quake, knocking the entire raid around. Triage anyone launched into fire and anyone who survives being knocked into Magtheridon's cleave. Do not stand so close to Magtheridon that Quake knocks you into cleave. Instant-cast spells work during Quake.
Conflagration
Conflagration places a small ring of fire on the ground, dealing Fire damage over time. Heal anyone with this DoT and avoid it yourself.
Phase three prep
Make sure the raid is as topped as possible going into Phase 3. Cave In will cause huge damage and kill anyone low.
Clickers and Backups
Primary clickers use their marker assignment. Backups cover missed or late clicks. Stay close to your cubes and move to them early. An addon like Deadly Boss Mods is crucial to understand timing on mechanics for this fight. Backups stay hyper aware of your clickers and be nearby just in case.
If clickers die
Refer to yourself in third person by saying your character's name is dead and the cube you were responsible for needs to be clicked. No one in the raid knows who "I" am, so be precise.
Quake into cleave deaths
It is not uncommon for melee and even ranged to get knocked by Quake directly into Magtheridon's cleave. Stay far enough back that even a few bad knocks in a row will not kill you.
Conflagration
Fire on the ground. Do not stand in it.
Lust after first quake
This ensures Lust happens early in the fight with the most people alive and for 100% of the time Magtheridon is banished, taking increased damage on the first click. Blow cooldowns the moment before Blast Nova goes out.
Phase 3 · 30% HP Cave In
Cave In
At 30% HP the ceiling caves in, causing 6k+ damage to every raider. This is a good time to use the healthstones you got before the pull.
Cave In
At 30% HP the ceiling caves in, causing 6k+ damage to every raider. This is a good time to use the healthstones you got before the pull. The raid will take their healthstones here as well, but quick healing is important to recover. Do not become so distracted healing raid damage that the tank dies; this frequently happens in this phase.
Cave In
At 30% HP the ceiling caves in, causing 6k+ damage to every raider. This is a good time to use the healthstones you got before the pull.
Channeler split
North tank and healer stay far from raid holding Square and Diamond up north. Raid kills Skull, X, and Moon down south. Do not take damage from both sides at once.
Cube clickers
Cubes are marked for clickers. Backups should always stay nearby as well. Stay aware of your surroundings and your partner. If main is not there to click, it automatically falls on backup.
Wipe Causes
  • Missed channeler kicks.
  • Cube click missed or late.
  • Tanks not holding marked channelers apart.
  • Hunter assigned to click does not deselect Magtheridon or face away and accidentally auto shoots, breaking the channel and wiping the raid.
  • Tank takes massive damage during and after Cave In but does not get the heals needed.
  • Losing players to preventable deaths from Conflagration, Cave In, and Cleave.
