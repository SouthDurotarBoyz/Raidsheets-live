Pull / Phase 1 · Spread and stabilize
Set Solarian cleanly
establishes Solarian's position immediately. Use the pull misdirect from Hunter, settle the boss, and avoid dragging her through spread ranged and healers.
Prepare steady raid healing
Main tank healers keep the tank stable while Raid healers spread with the raid and pre-position for constant raid damage and sudden Wrath movement.
Open carefully and stay spread
DPS opens after the tank position is stable and keeps safe spacing for Wrath of the Astromancer. Wrath target moves out immediately; everyone else holds spread.
Wrath of the Astromancer · move out
Do not chase Wrath
Keep Solarian controlled while the Wrath target leaves the raid. Do not pull the boss toward that player or force melee, healers, and ranged to collapse into the explosion path.
Keep the target alive moving
When Wrath is called, healers keep the target alive while they move out and recover anyone clipped by raid damage. Maintain your own spacing; do not stack near the Wrath target to land heals.
Wrath target leaves now
If you get Wrath of the Astromancer, move away from the raid immediately and call if needed. Everyone else holds spread positions and does not chase, follow, or stack near the target.
Add phase · agents and priests
Gather Solarium Agents
When Solarian disappears, moves to pickup zones, collects Solarium Agents quickly, and keeps them controlled so AoE can land without loose adds hitting healers.
Stabilize pickups and kicks
Cover off tank spikes as Agents are gathered, then watch players assigned to priests. Missed Solarium Priest casts and heals can stabilize the add wave, so keep core kickers alive while raid damage is rolling.
Priests are priority
AoE or kill Solarium Agents cleanly, but swap fast when Solarium Priests appear. Core kicks handle priest interrupts and control casts first; DPS tunnel on Agents only after priests are handled.
Final phase · Voidwalker burn
Fast transformation pickup
At low health, Solarian transforms into a Voidwalker-style final burn. The tank picks up the transformed boss quickly, faces it safely, and prevents panic movement from the previous add phase.
Stabilize tank and raid
Healers swap immediately from add cleanup to tank and raid stability. Use efficient cooldowns and fast recovery so the raid does not unravel during the transformation pickup.
Commit cooldowns cleanly
Once the transformed boss is picked up, DPS commits cooldowns and finishes. Keep curses active through the burn: Warlock, Warlock, and Warlock.
Solarian spread positioning

Add phase positioning
Add phase reference: off tank pickup zones, Solarium Agent control, priest interrupt positions, and where the raid should collapse or spread.
High Astromancer Solarian wipe causes
  • Wrath target does not move out and explodes in the raid.
  • Raid stacks too tightly during Wrath.
  • Off tank misses Solarium Agents during the disappear phase.
  • Core kicks are missed and priests stabilize the add wave.
  • DPS stays on the wrong target when priests spawn.
  • Healers fall behind during raid damage and add pickup.
  • Final phase pickup is slow after transformation.
