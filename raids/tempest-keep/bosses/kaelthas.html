Phase 1 · Advisors one at a time
Control assigned advisors
Advisors activate one at a time. , , and keep their advisors in the planned positions.
Keep Thaladred out of raid
When Thaladred gazes a player, the fixate target kites away from the raid path and tanks do not drag other advisors into that route.
Cover each tank handoff
Main tank healers, Off tank healers, and Raid healers track the active advisor and shift before the next one activates.
Stabilize fear and bomb damage
Sanguinar fear must be controlled, and Telonicus bombs or toys require quick spot healing without pulling players through unsafe advisor positions.
Respect dangerous positioning
Capernian is caster-tanked away from the raid, Sanguinar fear is controlled, and Telonicus bombs or toys are handled without dragging damage through the group.
Follow kill order cleanly
DPS burns the active advisor in order and does not cleave advisors into bad positions or force tanks to recover misplaced targets.
Phase 2 · Legendary weapons
Gather weapons fast
Weapons spawn together. Bow tanks gather the bows while controls the axe.
Call loose weapons
, , and help snap missed weapons back before they reach healers.
Prepare for tank spikes
Healers prepare for heavy tank damage while weapons are loose, then keep raid healing steady as weapon targets settle.
Protect weapon pickups
Keep the raid stable while players collect the weapons they need for the next phases.
Kill weapons cleanly
Raid kills weapons cleanly, then players collect the weapons their roles need before the next transition.
Confirm weapon collection
Players announce missed weapon pickups before the raid transitions into advisor control.
Phase 3 · Advisors return
Re-establish control immediately
Advisors revive together. The Sanguinar, Capernian, and Telonicus tanks re-establish control fast while Thaladred's gaze target leads him along the kite path.
Heal the split damage
Use legendary weapons properly and assign healing quickly as multiple advisors resume damage at once.
Call Mind Control targets
Mind control CC handles MCs immediately so healers are not killed or interrupted during the advisor return.
CC Mind Control instantly
Mind control CC is critical. Stop controlled players immediately, keep Capernian isolated, and keep moving if Thaladred gazes you.
Kill advisors in order
DPS kills advisors in the assigned order while using legendary weapons properly and avoiding cleave or threat that breaks tank control.
Phase 4 · Kael'thas active
Pick up Kael'thas cleanly
Kael'thas becomes active and holds him stable while the raid finishes the transition.
Control Phoenixes and eggs
controls Phoenixes away from raid, and eggs must die before Phoenixes revive.
Stabilize tank and raid
Main tank healers stabilize Kael'thas pickup while raid healers cover Phoenix, egg, and transition damage.
Control Mind Controls
Mind controls must be controlled immediately. Do not tunnel Kael'thas if a controlled player, Phoenix, or egg needs to die.
Kill eggs before revive
Swap to Phoenixes and eggs on call so they do not overwhelm the raid during Kael'thas burn windows.
Phase 5 · Gravity Lapse / final burn
Regroup after each lapse
During Gravity Lapse, spread in the air, avoid Nether Beam chains, and do not float too high or too close to others. After Gravity Lapse, regroup and continue burn.
Recover the raid calmly
Top players after each Gravity Lapse and keep enough coverage on any remaining Phoenix or egg assignments.
Spread, then finish
Avoid Nether Beam chains in the air, handle Phoenixes and eggs if alive, then finish cleanly without panic movement after the raid regroups.

Kael'thas Sunstrider wipe causes · don't
  • Thaladred gaze target runs through the raid.
