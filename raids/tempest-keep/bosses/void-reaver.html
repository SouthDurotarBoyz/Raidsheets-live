Pull · Positioning
Compete for Threat
Main tank sets Void Reaver in position while both off tanks build hard threat immediately. Knock Away can reduce the active tank's threat, so every tank must stay near the top and be ready for the boss to swap targets cleanly.
Heal all threat tanks
Each healer is assigned a tank to heal, but only one tank is taking damage at a time. Multiple healers should be on one tank, but expect tank damage to shift after Knock Away and do not tunnel one player while another tank climbs into the active threat position. Watch your threat tables to know who to heal next. Each healer is responsible for keeping their assigned tank alive.
Watch your threat
Hunters use assigned misdirects to stabilize opening threat. DPS should hold burst until tanks are established so Void Reaver does not turn into the raid after an early Knock Away.
Raid Stacks · Arcane Orb
Hold the boss steady
Hold Void Reaver steady while the stack and assigned ranged position for the Arcane Orbs. Only adjust if threat movement forces it, and never drag the boss through the stacked raid.
Stay with the raid
Heal from the stack, do not drift out unnecessarily, and spot-heal the assigned ranged orb dodgers if Arcane Orb clips them.
Arcane Orb positions
DPS stacks unless assigned as one of the ranged orb dodgers. Assigned ranged watch Arcane Orb, dodge the incoming path or impact, then reset to their assigned positions.
Pounding
Survive transitions
Maintain mitigation through Pounding windows and return to the boss quickly if knocked away.
Big heals
Pounding deals heavy raid-wide AoE damage near Void Reaver. Pre-load raid healing, use cooldowns to keep tanks covered through heavy raid damage from Pounding, and still spot-heal orb dodgers as needed.
Do not panic during Pounding
Use personals or healthstones during Pounding to help healers. Stacked group stays planted and assigned ranged orb dodgers reset positions after each dodge.
Void Reaver stack positioning
Everyone stacks except the assigned ranged. They watch the incoming Arcane Orb, dodge out of its path, then reset to their positions.
Wipe Causes
  • One of the assigned ranged fails to dodge Arcane Orb.
  • Orb dodgers do not reset after moving.
  • The stacked raid panics and scatters instead of holding position.
  • Tanks lose threat after Knock Away and Void Reaver turns into the raid.
  • Healers fall behind during Pounding.
  • DPS pulls threat before tanks stabilize.
  • Raiders ignore personals or healthstones during Pounding.
