Pull / Phase 1 · Platform rotation
Cover every landing
and pre-position and move early. Al'ar must always have a tank in melee range when landed.
No melee tank = Flame Buffet
If a platform is empty when Al'ar lands, Flame Buffet stacks on the raid and can quickly wipe the pull.
Track platform tanks
Main tank healers keep range on active platform tanks as they rotate, then stabilize the next landing immediately.
Light raid triage
Raid healers cover incidental platform, Ember, and movement damage without drifting onto unsafe upper ledges.
Ranged from center
Ranged can focus Al'ar from center or ground positions while tanks handle the platform rotation above.
Melee handle Ember adds safely
Swap to Ember adds as assigned, keep them controlled away from loose players, and avoid cleaving them into the raid.
Flame Quills / platform danger
Jump down on Flame Quills
When Al'ar flies to center and starts Flame Quills, platform tanks leave the upper platforms immediately or die fast.
Re-cover before the next land
After Quills ends, move back up early and re-cover landing platforms before Al'ar chooses the next perch.
Do not heal from upper platforms
Drop with the tanks when Flame Quills starts. Anyone remaining upstairs can die before heals land.
Clear upper platforms
Stop tunneling and get down as soon as Flame Quills begins. Resume damage after tanks safely re-cover the landing points.
Phase transition / Rebirth
Fast boss pickup
At 0% in Phase 1, Al'ar casts Rebirth into Phase 2. and backup tanks get threat and face the boss quickly.
Prepare for heavier damage
Pre-position away from center, then shift to heavier tank and raid healing as Phase 2 starts.
Move away from Rebirth
When Phase 1 ends, clear the center and landing area so tanks can pick up Al'ar without extra raid damage or body-blocking.

Keep add tank alive
Add tank healers stay ready for Ember pickup spikes while the raid is moving out of Dive Bomb.
Heal the move, then spread
Raid healers cover Dive Bomb movement and Flame Patch mistakes, then spread back out with the raid.
Never stand in Flame Patch

Kill Ember adds away from raid
During Dive Bomb, stack or move as assigned, then spread back out. Burn Ember adds only in the safe area because they explode on death.
