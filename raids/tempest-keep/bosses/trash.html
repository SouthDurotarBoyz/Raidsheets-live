Al'ar Trash Field Guide

Astromancer

Kill target 1
  • Molten ArmorPurgeableDeals Fire damage to attackers.
  • Blast WaveFire AoE and slow.
  • Fireball VolleyInstant Fire damage to nearby players.

How to handleKill early with Star Scryer; both are dangerous raid-damage mobs.

Star Scryer

Kill target 2
  • DominationMind ControlMind controls a raid player; crowd control them immediately.
  • Arcane BlastArcane damage and knockback; tanks keep their back to a wall.
  • StarfallInstant Arcane damage to nearby players.

How to handleA dangerous raid-damage mob. Polymorph first while killing Astromancer; be ready to re-sheep after Vindicator Cleanse.

Blood Warder Vindicator

Kill target 3
  • Hammer of JusticeMagic dispelSix-second magical stun on highest threat.
  • Flash of LightCannot interruptFast heal on friendly targets.
  • Hammer of WrathTargets players at 20% health or lower.
  • CleanseRemoves friendly magical debuffs, including Polymorph.

How to handleWatch for Cleanse breaking sheep, dispel stuns, and keep pressure on priority targets.

Bloodwarder Legionnaire

Kill target 4
  • WhirlwindChanneled 360-degree AoE with heavy damage at the end.
  • CleaveFrontal attack hitting up to three targets.
  • BloodthirstHeavy weapon-damage physical attack.

How to handleSplit Legionnaires and Vindicators evenly; face cleaves away and move out for Whirlwind.

Bloodwarder Marshal

Kill target 1
  • WhirlwindPhysical damage to nearby players.
  • BloodthirstPhysical damage with a small self-heal.
  • UppercutPhysical damage and tank knockback.

How to handleIn its patrol with two Squires, split Marshal and Squires between tanks. Face Marshal into a wall; it stays still during Whirlwind.

Bloodwarder Squire

Kill target 2
  • Flash of Light1.5-second heal on friendly targets.
Cannot interrupt

How to handleAppears near the Bloodwarder Marshal pack. One tank can pick up the Marshal while the other grabs the two Squires. Not very dangerous, but keep the pack controlled and kill cleanly.

Phoenix-Hawk Hatchling

Kill target 1
  • SilenceSix-second AoE silence; spellcasters stay back.
  • Wing BuffetKnocks back nearby players.

How to handlePull into the hallway and tank in a corner. Tempest Falconers shoot through walls; keep them in melee range or outrange them to force movement.

Tempest Falconer

Kill target 2
  • Ranged shotsFalconers can shoot through line of sight.

How to handleNot as dangerous as the hatchlings. Keep the Falconer in melee range or outrange it to force it to move. A second tank can get aggro and drag it into the pack by staying in melee range.

Phoenix-Hawk

Kill target 1
  • DiveCharges the furthest player and knocks back nearby players.
  • Mana BurnAoE mana burn and damage to players with mana.

How to handleStack everyone but the second tank in a corner. The second tank gets on threat, then moves out to absorb Dive.

Kill target 1
  • CounterchargeArcane auto-attack damage and a nearby silence.
  • Knock AwayKnocks back highest threat and resets threat.

How to handleKeep melee behind and ranged at maximum range. Tank into a corner and taunt back after Knock Away.

  • OverchargeSpell ReflectionMassive Arcane burst on highest-threat tank.

How to handleMelee stay behind and ranged stay at max range. Prepare for heavy melee damage; taunt or rapidly recover after Overcharge. Protection Warriors can Spell Reflect it.

Mind Control

Crimson Hand Blood Knight

Kill target 2
  • Flash of LightCannot interruptHeal on a friendly target.
  • Hammer of JusticeDispellableStun that can be dispelled.
  • RenewPurgeableHeal over time.

Crimson Hand Centurion

Kill target 3
  • Arcane FlurryMassive damage to a random target.
Crowd control can stop casts

How to handleA dangerous raid-damage source. Polymorph or Gouge prevents casts; AoE packs once tank threat is stable.

Crimson Hand Inquisitor

Kill target 1
  • Mind FlayShadow damage that slows movement.
  • Shadow Word: PainShadow damage over time.
  • Power InfusionBuffs the target's spell damage and healing.
PolymorphShadow damage

How to handleCrowd control if needed; Polymorph works. Watch for Shadow damage and remove or manage pressure quickly. Treat it as a caster and control threat in the Kael'Thas trash area.
