Trash Kill Priority
  1. Shadowy NecromancerKill first; interrupt and stun casts.
  2. BansheeHandle after Necromancers; decurse, and interrupt only with spare kicks.
  3. Frost Wyrm / GargoyleRanged focus; use line of sight and pull them down as needed.
  4. AbominationOff-tanks control them; tanks manage the stuns.
  5. Infernal / Giant InfernalTank, stun or banish if needed, then burn.
  6. Crypt FiendKeep controlled and in melee; cleave down.
  7. GhoulCleave down; Enrage is not a major threat.
  8. Fel Hound / Fel StalkerBanish if needed and kill last.
Trash NPC Field Guide
Ghoul

Ghoul

Low / cleave
  • CannibalizeAt low health, runs to a corpse and heals over time.
  • EnrageTranq ShotGains heavy melee attack speed and grows larger.

How to handleCleave them down. Enrage is not a major threat; Tranq Shot can remove it if needed.

Crypt Fiend

Crypt Fiend

Low / keep in melee
  • WebRootRoots a target.
  • Crypt ScarabsSummons scarabs, especially when the primary threat target is out of melee.

How to handleUse line of sight and keep the threat target in melee. Clean positioning prevents scarabs from becoming a problem.

Necromancer

Shadowy Necromancer

Highest
  • Shadow BoltInterruptibleShadow nuke.
  • Unholy FrenzyDispellableWorks on MCBuffs attack speed while dealing damage over time.
  • CrippleInterruptibleDispellableSlows, increases attack delay, and lowers strength.
  • Raise DeadSummons skeleton adds.

How to handleKill first. Stagger casts so the tank is not hit by everything at once. Stuns and interrupts work; Grounding Totem helps.

Abomination

Abomination

Medium / tank controlled
  • Disease CloudShort-range Nature damage aura.
  • KnockdownStuns the primary target and deals physical damage.

How to handleOff-tanks pick up one or two when needed. Tanks can use Ironshield Potions for armor or Free Action Potions to avoid the stun.

Banshee

Banshee

High after Necromancers
  • Banshee CurseDecursableReduces melee and ranged hit chance.
  • Anti-Magic ShellAbsorbs a large amount of magic damage.
  • Banshee WailInterruptibleShadow nuke.

How to handleKeep decurses going. Interrupt when possible, but reserve priority kicks for Necromancers. Once they die, melee can kill through Anti-Magic Shell.

Gargoyle

Gargoyle

Ranged / wave-dependent
  • Gargoyle StrikeDeals Nature damage to an enemy.

How to handleGet aggro and pull them down. Line of sight helps greatly; ranged should clean them up while active.

Frost Wyrm

Frost Wyrm

Ranged focus
  • Frost BreathSlow dispellableDeals Frost damage and slows the target and nearby players.

How to handleGet aggro and pull it down. Ranged focus it; melee can connect near the ground. Spread enough to avoid breath splash.
