Alliance Camp NPC Field Guide

Shadowy Necromancer

Kill target 1
  • Shadow BoltInterruptibleShadow nuke.
  • Unholy FrenzyDispellableWorks on MCBuffs attack speed while dealing damage over time.
  • CrippleInterruptibleDispellableSlows, increases attack delay, and lowers strength.
  • Raise DeadSummons skeleton adds.

How to handleKill first. Stagger casts so the tank is not hit by everything at once. Stuns and interrupts work; Grounding Totem helps.

Banshee

Kill target 2
  • Banshee CurseDecursableReduces melee and ranged hit chance.
  • Anti-Magic ShellAbsorbs a large amount of magic damage.
  • Banshee WailInterruptibleShadow nuke.

How to handleHandle after Necromancers and keep decurses going. Interrupt only when kicks are spare; melee can kill through Anti-Magic Shell.

Abomination

Kill target 3
  • Disease CloudShort-range Nature damage aura.
  • KnockdownStuns the primary target and deals physical damage.

How to handleOff-tanks control one or two as needed. Tanks manage Knockdown stuns and can use Ironshield or Free Action Potions.

Crypt Fiend

Kill target 4
  • WebRootRoots a target.
  • Crypt ScarabsSummons scarabs, especially when the primary threat target is out of melee.

How to handleUse line of sight and keep the threat target in melee. Clean positioning prevents scarabs from becoming a problem.

Ghoul

Kill target 5
  • CannibalizeAt low health, runs to a corpse and heals over time.
  • EnrageTranq ShotGains heavy melee attack speed and grows larger.

How to handleCleave them down. Enrage is not a major threat; Tranq Shot can remove it if needed.
