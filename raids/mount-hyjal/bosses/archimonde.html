Wipe Causes
  • Players miss Tears after Air Burst.
  • Grip of the Legion is not decursed quickly.
  • Doomfire is dragged through the raid.
  • Fear sends players into bad positions.
  • Deaths cascade and wipe the raid.
  • When a Druid, Shaman or Hunter dies raiders nearby lose 2250 mana and take 4500 Nature damage over 8 sec.
  • When a Warrior, Rogue or Paladin dies nearby raiders take 4500 damage and increase their damage taken by 50% for 8 sec.
  • When a Priest, Mage or Warlock dies nearby raiders take 4500 Fire damage silencing them for 6 sec.
  • Archimonde will cast Finger of Death when no players are in melee range of him. It will hit for 20k DPS and wipe the raid.
  • Shamans do not drop tremor totems or Main Tank loses fear ward.
