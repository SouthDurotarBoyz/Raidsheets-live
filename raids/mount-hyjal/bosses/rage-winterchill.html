Phase 1 · Spread and react
Hold Rage steady
Set the boss in the center and avoid unnecessary movement. The off tank stays ready to stabilize Rage if the main tank is stunned by Icebolt.
React to Icebolt
Assigned Icebolt healers immediately focus the stunned target through its heavy damage. Keep tank and raid coverage while everyone remains spread.
Spread and move
Spread around the boss. Use a stun break or PvP trinket if Icebolt targets you, and leave Death & Decay immediately.
Critical mechanics
Icebolt
Stuns one player and deals heavy damage over a short duration. Assigned healers focus the target; stun breaks and PvP trinkets can end the stun early.
Death & Decay
Creates a large damaging ground effect. Everyone inside moves out immediately.
Frost Nova
Roots players within 20 yards. It can be dispelled, but prioritize removing it when a rooted player must escape Death & Decay.
Frost Armor
Slows attackers who strike Rage. It can be dispelled, but is not a primary assignment.
