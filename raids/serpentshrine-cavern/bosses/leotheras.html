Phase 1 · Humanoid Form (~45 seconds)
MT on Leotheras
Main tank holds boss. Off tank ready for Whirlwind reset. Leotheras is immune to taunt.
Whirlwind drops threat
After every Whirlwind, threat is reset. Burst threat immediately when it ends.
Whirlwind positioning
Leotheras targets a random player and spins toward them. Reposition if you're targeted or in between; otherwise stay still.
Whirlwind bleed DoT
Anyone hit takes heavy damage and a bleed DoT. Only Divine Shield, Blessing of Protection, or Ice Block remove the DoT. Heal through it otherwise.
Watch threat after Whirlwind
Threat resets. If you heal too aggressively before tank locks aggro, Leotheras turns to you. Pause heals briefly.
Pre-position for transition
Demon Form starts after ~45s. Demon tank healers prep cooldowns, Chaos Blast ramps fast.
Stop DPS before Whirlwind ends
Threat resets when Whirlwind finishes channeling. Stop attacks and dots before it ends or you rip threat.
Stay spread, run if targeted
Ranged spread out. If Leotheras spins toward you, run far enough that he loses interest and picks a new target. If you are in the path of Leotheras spinning, move out the way.
Melee disengage on Whirlwind
Get out of melee the instant Whirlwind starts.
Transition · Humanoid to Demon Form
Demon tank takes Leo
Demon tank pulls Demon Leotheras away to the right area. Distance keeps Chaos Blast splash off the raid.
Switch to Demon tank healing
Demon tank healers on Demon tank. Each Chaos Blast hits harder than the last. Stack heals and shields. Trinkets and cooldowns at six to eight stacks and beyond is best.
Threat Reset
Hard stop on DPS dots should stop sooner. Misdirect onto Demon tank. Resume DPS only when demon tank has threat.
Phase 2 · Demon Form (~60 seconds)
Chaos Blast on Demon tank
Stacking fire damage debuff. Damage starts low, ramps to lethal. Demon tank survives roughly 12–14 stacks before death, the phase ends well before that. Mitigation is best saved for high damage ramp.
Tank Demon away from raid
Demon tank holds Leotheras on the right area, well away from the left side where the raid sits. Chaos Blast splashes nearby targets for massive damage, distance keeps melee safe. If Demon Tank is melee max range for the tank and melee dps creates enough space for safe DPS.
Demon tank damage ramps fast
Each Chaos Blast hits harder than the last. Stack heals and shields. Trinkets and cooldowns at six to eight stacks and beyond is best.
Insidious Whispers - Kill your Inner Demon
About 25 seconds into phase, five random non-aggro players get Insidious Whisper. If you're targeted, switch off the boss and kill your own demon immediately. No one else can damage it for you. If you fail to kill it in time you will be mind controlled for ten minutes.
Insidious Whispers - Kill your Inner Demon
About 25 seconds into phase, five random non-aggro players get Insidious Whisper. If you're targeted, switch off the boss and kill your own demon immediately. No one else can damage it for you. If you fail to kill it in time you will be mind controlled for ten minutes.
Demon Form Pump
Demon Form damage is mostly tank-only. Free DPS window to burn the boss while watching for Inner Demon.
Transition · Demon to Humanoid Form
Main tank bursts threat
After demon form Leotheras transitions back to human. Tanks must burst threat and pick up agro quickly. Leotheras cannot be taunted. Cycles back to phase one humanoid Leotheras mechanics.
Switch back to main tank healing
Drop Demon tank focus. Cycles back to phase one humanoid Leotheras mechanics. Humanoid and Demon switch every 45–60s until 15% HP.
Verify ALL Inner Demons dead first
If any Inner Demon is still alive when Leotheras returns to humanoid, that player is Mind Controlled for 10 minutes. Confirm all five are dead before transition.
Threat Reset
Hard stop on DPS dots should stop sooner. Misdirect onto Leotheras's Humanoid tank. Resume DPS only when humanoid tank has threat.
Phase 3 · Split Form at 15% HP
Both forms active simultaneously
Main tank on Humanoid left side. Demon tank on Demon right side. Keep them separated.
Kill Humanoid and Demon despawns
Focus all DPS on the Humanoid form. When it dies, Demon form vanishes too. Do not DPS the demon at all here.
Inner Demons still trigger
Insidious Whisper mechanics resume in split phase. Watch your debuffs and kill your own demon if debuffed with Insidious Whispers.
Cooldowns
Chaotic phase with all damage sources active at the same time. Save major mana and healing tools for here if available. Heal through and count on DPS.
Bloodlust
Pop everything and kill Humanoid form fast. Every second of split phase is more Inner Demons and stacking Chaos Blasts.
Inner Demons still trigger
Insidious Whisper mechanics resume in split phase. Watch your debuffs and kill your own demon if debuffed with Insidious Whispers.
Humanoid positioning
Main tank holds Leotheras on the upper platform. Melee on boss; ranged and healers spread in a wide arc.
Demon positioning
Demon tank holds Demon Leotheras at the lower-right platform edge to keep Chaos Blast splash away from the raid.
Split positioning
At 15%, main tank keeps Humanoid on the upper platform while Demon tank pulls Demon away. Burn Humanoid.
Wipe Causes
  • Pet kills your Inner Demon and you get Mind Controlled for 10 minutes anyway.
  • Inner Demon left alive and the player is Mind Controlled for 10 minutes.
  • Heal or DPS through Whirlwind threat reset ripping threat resulting in instant death.
  • Leftover trash in room, Whirlwind pulls adds, raid wipes.
  • Standing too close to the warlock tank and Chaos Blast AoE's the raid.
  • Standing in the path of whirlwind and taking massive damage ticks.
