Wipe Causes
  • DPS rips threat on transition. Dot's from the previous phase can pull enough agro to rip, especially Doom.
  • Massive HPS pulls threat on the adds or Hydross during transition.
  • Push Mark of Hydross past five stacks and the tank gets one-shot.
  • Cluster within 8 yards in Frost form and Water Tomb chain wipes the raid.
  • Adds are not controlled and kill raiders.
