Phase 1 · 100% to 25% · Earthquake every ~40s spawns 12 murlocs
Morogrim faced away from raid
Hold Morogrim at the south east pillar by the door, facing him away from the raid so Tidal Wave hits no one.
Tidal Wave a front cone
4–5k frost damage plus 400% attack speed slow for 15s in a frontal cone. Main tank should be the only one hit.
Earthquake spawns 12 murlocs
Heavy raid AoE damage. Six murlocs come from each entrance; paladins at north and south doorways gather their packs and frost traps slow them.
Paladin divine shield save
If a paladin gets Watery Grave during murlocs, Divine Shield drops aggro but saves them. The other paladin or raid AoE covers while they re-establish.
Watery Healthstones
Use a Healthstone if low after Watery Grave so fall damage does not become fatal.
Watery Grave · 4 random players
Targets are teleported to one of 4 grave spots, stunned 6s, and take roughly 6k frost plus fall damage. Watery Grave healer stands nearby, in range of all 4 spots.
Watery Healthstones
Use a Healthstone if low after Watery Grave so fall damage does not become fatal.
After Earthquake watch threat
Murlocs will kill healers if massive raid healing rips threat before paladin tanks can corral all 12 murlocs.
Extra Healers
This is a fight where 6 healing is acceptable. This is SSC's heaviest healing fight, and more healers distribute threat after Earthquake.
After earthquake wait for paladins
Do not AoE murlocs until both paladins have aggro on their packs. Early AoE rips murlocs and wipes raids.
Hunters Frost Traps before each Earthquake
North hunter drops trap at north door. South hunter drops trap at south door. Traps slow murlocs and give paladins gathering time.
Murlocs first then boss
Switch to murlocs as the paladins establish threat. Boss DPS resumes only after all 12 are dead, murlocs hit hard if left alive.
Stay out of front cone
Only the Morogrim tank should be in front. Tidal Wave plus 400% attack speed slow eats melee that strafe wrong.
Phase 2 · 25% to 0% · Tank Moves to Back Corner
Move boss to southeast corner
At 25%, Morogrim tank drags Morogrim to the southeast corner near the entrance. Raid follows. No more Earthquake, Watery Grave, Murlocs from here.
Paladins free for boss DPS
No more murloc waves. North paladin and South paladin can DPS. Tidal Wave still applies on Morogrim tank.
Watery Grave healers raid heal
No more Watery Grave casts. Watery Grave healer(s) rejoin raid healing in the back corner.
If targeted by Globule: kite
Whole raid stacks in the back right near the entrance. Globules spawn from waterfalls below; distance gives time for them to despawn before reaching anyone. If one does reach you run away.
Stack on upper platform
Whole raid stacks in the back right near the entrance. Globules spawn from waterfalls below; distance gives time for them to despawn before reaching anyone. If one does reach you run away. They explode for 4–6k frost AoE on contact.
If targeted by Globule: kite
Four globules spawn from waterfalls below, fixate random players, and approach slowly. They explode for 4–6k frost AoE on contact, run away from raid and let distance despawn them after roughly 30s.
Bloodlust window
25% to 0% is the burn. No murloc interruptions. Pop everything and finish him.
Phase 1 · positioning
Morogrim tank holds Morogrim east. Paladin off-tanks at north and south doorways with Frost Traps down. Watery Grave healer stands in range of all four grave spots. Murlocs spawn from doorways, get trapped, and paladins gather.
Phase 2 · upper platform
At 25%, Morogrim tank drags Morogrim to the back right near the entrance. Raid stacks behind the boss. Globules spawn from waterfalls below and travel slowly but distance gives them time to despawn. If one does reach you run away. They explode for 4–6k frost AoE on contact.
Morogrim wipe causes · don't
  • Heal raid before murlocs are tanked — healers pull aggro and die.
  • AoE before paladins lock threat — same death, raid-wide.
  • Stand in front of Morogrim — Tidal Wave cone reduces attack speed by 400%.
  • Forget Frost Trap before Earthquake — murlocs reach raid before paladin gathers.
  • Let Water Globule reach the raid — AoE 4–6k frost on everyone close.
  • Forget Healthstone after Watery Grave — fall damage finishes you.
