Phase 1 · Combat (~90 seconds)
Main Tank Positioning
Main Tank must stay in Lurker's hitbox. Lurker will cast Water Bolt that one-shots cloth if no one is in range.
Whirl recovery is critical
Whirl knocks the tank back. Main Tank can walk forward into the water before it hits to get knocked back onto the platform instead of away. If you get knocked run back in immediately.
Spout
You will see, "takes a deep breath" before Spout. This is the warning. Rotate around the boss avoiding the beam and continue building threat in melee. After Spout ends, expect an immediate Whirl.
Whirl
Roughly 3500 physical to everyone in melee. Heavy main tank pressure, keep the main tank topped between knockbacks.
Spout
You will see, "takes a deep breath" before Spout. This is the warning. Jump into the water and let it pass overhead, you can still heal underwater. Anyone caught takes huge damage and a knockback. Heal clipped raiders fast, they will take burn damage swimming back and they may land out of line of sight.
Geyser
Random target AoE knockup within 10 yards. Do not group too close and top off affected players.
Spout
You will see, "takes a deep breath" before Spout. This is the warning. Melee rotate around the boss avoiding the beam. Ranged jump into the water and let it pass overhead, you can still DPS underwater. If you go into the water you will take burn damage, don't stay too long. React immediately, every time.
Whirl
Melee knocked back must get back fast. Save Intercept, Charge, or Feral Charge for this.
Stay spread 10 yards
Geyser hits everyone within 10 yards of the target.
Phase 2 · Submerged (60 seconds)
Both tanks on center platform
Main tank and off tank both stay on the central platform. Kill adds, Guardians, spawn around the ring and path inward from outer platforms toward center.
Split center pickups
Main tank takes single Guardian by Platform Three and tanks by the edge of the platform for ranged to hit. Off Tank takes two Guardians by Platforms One and Two then tanks them nearby so ranged can attack.
Main tank ready for emerge
Lurker resurfaces at 60 seconds. Main tank must finish adds and reposition fast, the first Spout fires roughly 5 seconds after emerge.
Both Tanks On Adds
Main tank and off tank both take damage on center platform. Main tank healers and off tank healers stay center to cover both. Outer platforms are covered by raid healers.
Ambusher multi-shot
Coilfang Ambushers hit outer platforms with multi-shot damage. Make sure outer platforms have heal coverage.
Damage spike on emerge
Lurker comes back with an instant Whirl into Spout. Be ready to heal Main tank and any melee taking damage from the whirl.
Nine adds in 60 seconds
Six Coilfang Ambushers, two per outer platform, and three Coilfang Guardians on center. Hard timer means any not killed continue fighting into Lurker's emerge.
Each outer platform: Kills both adds
Each platform is assigned a CC marker. The DPS on their platforms use their marker to signal which mob is CC'd by that group. Once the ambushers arrive on each platform DPS mark one with their group's marker. The unmarked ambusher dies first while each ranged group maintains CC on the marked ambusher. Once one unmarked ambusher dies ranged turn on the central platform guardian add. Once the guardian is dead, ranged finish their marked CC. The kill order for ranged in this phase is Unmarked Ambusher, Guardian, Marked CC'd Ambusher.
Melee on guardians
Both tanks hold guardian adds in the center while melee burns them down. Guardians can cleave for massive damage so stay behind them. Use stuns to help the tanks.
Stay on your assigned platform
Do not drift between outer platforms.
Watch your threat
Hold off on Lurker for a few globals when he resurfaces and let the main tank get threat before first Spout.
Lurker resurfaces · Phase 1 repeats
Phase 1 positioning
Center platform: melee and main tank on Lurker. Outer platforms: ranged spread. Do not stand within 10 yards of another raider — Geyser hits everyone in range.
Phase 2 add positioning
Both tanks on center platform holding their adds. Each outer platform kills the marked add while CCers hold the second. Ranged then helps melee with guardians before finishing their marked add.
Wipe causes
  • Tank leaves melee range.
  • Spout knocks back raiders causing damage and far knockback into burning water.
  • Ambusher CC breaks or is not applied on a platform, that platform wipes.
  • DPS Lurker before main tank locks threat on emerge.
