Stage 1 · Kill Tidalvess the Shaman · Spitfire Totem priority
Tank positions on pull
Tanks pull the boss and adds into position based on the map to the right. Caribdis must be taken far out of LoS to stop her heals.
Tidalvess Frost Shock + Windfury
Tidalvess deals heavy frost burst to the Tidalvess tank. Grounding Totem in the Tidalvess group helps if a shaman is available.
Cataclysmic Bolt
Karathress targets a random mana user, deals 50% max HP shadow damage, and stuns. This is a real danger to paladin tanks.
Cataclysmic Bolt · 50% max HP
Karathress targets a random mana user, deals 50% max HP shadow damage, and stuns. Keep the entire raid above 50% HP at all times.
Spitfire Totem AoE
Tidalvess summons Spitfire Totem. It deals raid-wide damage until killed. Heavy AoE healing while DPS swaps to it.
Spitfire Totem first priority kill
If Spitfire Totem appears, switch to it instantly. It will wipe the raid in seconds. Killing it fast is not an option.
Focus Tidalvess
All DPS on Tidalvess except any assigned Karathress threat-keepers. Tidalvess totems are the worst raid damage.
75% rule on Karathress
Karathress must stay above 75% HP until all three guards are dead. Below 75% with guards alive triggers Blessing of the Tides and wipes the raid.
Life Tap with care
Cataclysmic Bolt hitting a low HP warlock kills. When you lifetap don't do it much at once.
Curse of Tongues
Warlocks Curse of Tongues on Caribdis and maintain it the entire fight. The warlock maintaining Curse of Tongues can stay on that side of the room and DPS Caribdis.
Stage 2 · Kill Sharkkis · Karathress now has Spitfire Totem
Karathress now drops Spitfire Totem
When Tidalvess dies, Karathress gains Spitfire Totem. Karathress tank watches for it spawning at Karathress's feet and drags boss away from raid if needed.
Sharkkis pet damage
Sharkkis summons pets: elemental or Sporebat. Tank should be ready for pet pressure and messy cleave.
Cataclysmic Bolt continues
Keep mana users above 50% HP and spot heal the affected player.
Spitfire Totem now drops on boss too
Two potential Spitfire spawns now. AoE healing increases.
Switch to Sharkkis
All DPS to Sharkkis. Pets die fast, but focus boss unless the pet is causing immediate danger. Spitfire Totems still take priority over everything else.
Sharkkis pets
Elemental pet explodes on death. Clear melee when killing it. Sporebat does not explode.
Karathress still at 75%+
Maintain threat only on Karathress. Do not push him below 75%.
Stage 3 · DPS split targets · Karathress now has Spitfire + Beast Within
Karathress hits much harder
Karathress now has Beast Within periodically. Karathress tank should have defensive cooldowns ready when boss enrages.
Tidal Surge from Caribdis
Caribdis casts a 10-yard AoE freeze every 10–15 seconds. Keep Caribdis isolated and controlled.
Karathress tank spikes harder
Beast Within increases Karathress damage. Keep Karathress tank stable while continuing Cataclysmic Bolt and Spitfire coverage.
Tidal Surge risk
Anyone near Caribdis can be frozen. Spot heal frozen players if they get clipped.
Spitfire Totem still priority damage
Raid damage continues until each totem dies.
Cover raid split
Healers move to cover the split in raid positioning. Some to ranged at Caribdis others to melee on Karathress. Melee side will take more damage here.
DPS split
Ranged and casters handle Caribdis. Melee target Karathress.
Spitfire Totem still priority
Kill Spitfire Totems immediately.
Interrupt Caribdis at range
Casters can interrupt her from range but avoid melee range.
Stage 4 · Kill Karathress · all guards dead
Karathress has stolen abilities
By now Karathress has Spitfire Totem and Beast Within, and Caribdis AoE. Karathress tank holds boss and uses defensive cooldowns during Beast Within.
Cooldowns
Keep raid above 50% for Cataclysmic Bolt. Keep Karathress tank stable during Beast Within. Heal through Spitfire Totem damage while DPS kills it.
Spitfire Totem still priority
Kill Spitfire Totems immediately.
Burn Karathress
All DPS swaps to Karathress after all guards are dead. Spitfire Totem still dies first.
Stage 1 pull positions
Tidalvess top, Sharkkis right, Caribdis isolated bottom-left, Karathress center-bottom. All four tanks separated before pull.
Stage 3 Caribdis positioning
Caribdis isolated bottom-left, Karathress in his spot. Casters DPS Caribdis from range. Melee stay off Caribdis unless assigned because Tidal Surge freezes them.
Wipe Causes
  • Push Karathress below 75% with guards alive he triggers Blessing of the Tides and triples his damage.
  • Miss a Spitfire Totem and raid-wide AoE damage kills the raid in seconds.
  • Let Cataclysmic Bolt hit a low-HP mana user for 50% max HP shadow and they die.
  • Melee Caribdis unnecessarily she Tidal Surge freezes them to death.
  • Put a paladin on Karathress, Tidalvess, or Sharkkis. The Cataclysmic Bolt punishes mana users.
  • Let the adds run free to kill DPS and healers.
