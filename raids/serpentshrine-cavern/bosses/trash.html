A. Pre-Hydross Trash
Local priority
  1. Coilfang Hate-Screamer or Serpentshrine Sporebats, depending on raid preference.
  2. Coilfang Beast-Tamer.
  3. Underbog Colossus mechanics / death adds.
  4. Remaining bats or minor adds.
Beast-Tamer pack
Main tank normally picks up Coilfang Beast-Tamer and faces it away because it cleaves. Off tank(s) pick up the other mobs and hold Sporebats or Hate-Screamers steady for the kill order.
Underbog Colossus calls
Underbog Colossus rolls different ability pairs and death effects. Call what it rolls, keep it controlled, and have the off tank gather any Ragers or other death adds for quick cleave.
Cleanse and move
Initial Infection is a disease DoT and should be cleansed quickly. Targeted Acid Geyser players run to the tank / Colossus position so the spray does not hit the raid.
Spore Quake / Toxic Pool
Spore Quake has very large range, so move out fast. Toxic Pool is ground poison; move out and keep healing mobile players.
Pick silence or charge first
Raid may kill Sporebats first or Hate-Screamers first depending on whether charge stuns or silence is the bigger issue. Hate-Screamer uses Sonic Scream near itself and a 5 second AoE Silence.
Burn Bestial Wrath
Beast-Tamer uses Bestial Wrath, increasing its damage and empowering the two Sporebats. Sporebats charge the furthest target, stun, and apply Spore Burst Nature splash plus DoT.
B. Pre-Lurker Trash
Local priority
  1. Coilfang Priestess.
  2. Coilfang Shatterer.
  3. Vashj’ir Honor Guard.
  4. Greyheart Technician.
  5. Remaining stacked trash.
Honor Guard positioning
Vashj’ir Honor Guard has Enrage / Frenzy, melee-range Frightening Shout, Knockback, and Mortal Cleave. Face Honor Guards away and use wall or pillar positioning when possible.
Stacked platform packs
These platform packs above Lurker are usually stacked and AoE’d, but tanks need time to establish threat before DPS opens. Greyheart Technician applies Hamstring.
Priestess and Spirit
Coilfang Priestess casts Holy Smite and Holy Fire; dispel Holy Fire. On death she spawns a Spirit that looks like Spirit of Redemption but does not mean a player priest died, and it spams Holy Nova to damage the raid and heal enemies.
Fear support
Tremor Totem and Fear Ward help stabilize Honor Guard fear. Keep tanks topped before Knockback and Mortal Cleave overlap.
Control Shatter Armor
Coilfang Shatterer applies Shatter Armor, reducing tank armor by 50% for 15 seconds. Grounding Totem can prevent Shatter Armor, then kill Shatterer after Priestess.
AoE after control
Wait for tank threat and priority targets before broad AoE. Cleave the remaining stacked trash only after Priestess Spirit and Shatterer pressure are handled.
C. Pre-Leotheras Trash
Local priority
  1. Greyheart Nether-Mage if not sheeped.
  2. Greyheart Tidecaller.
  3. Water Elemental Totem immediately when it appears.
  4. Greyheart Skulker.
  5. Greyheart Shield-Bearer.
  6. Coilfang Fathom-Witch.
  7. Coilfang Serpentguard.
  8. Banished Serpentshrine Lurker last.
Sheep or kill Nether-Mage
Greyheart Nether-Mage is highly dangerous but can be Polymorphed. If Nether-Mages are sheeped, kill Greyheart Tidecaller first; otherwise remove the Nether-Mage before it chains instant spells.
Shield-Bearer stack
Greyheart Shield-Bearer uses Shield Charge and Avenger’s Shield. Raid should stack behind Shield-Bearers to prevent charge behavior while tanks keep packs stable.
Fathom-Witch positioning
Coilfang Fathom-Witch uses Domination, Shadow Bolt, and Shadow Nova knockback. Position carefully because Shadow Nova can throw players into dangerous water and fish areas.
Purge and dispel Tidecaller
Greyheart Tidecaller Poison Shield should be purged or removed with Arcane Shot. Virulent Poison can be dispelled, and Water Elemental Totem must die quickly while the elemental itself is ignored.
Control Domination
Domination can be prevented or absorbed with Grounding Totem. Controlled players should be controlled quickly until the effect ends.
Kill totem, not elemental
Tidecaller drops Water Elemental Totem; kill the totem immediately when it appears and ignore the elemental itself. Purge, dispel, and swap back to the priority target.
Banish Lurkers last
Serpentshrine Lurkers should be banished and saved for the end; do not fight them with other mobs active. Move away from mushrooms and do not waste time killing mushrooms.
Fathom-Witch then Serpentguard
For Fathom-Witch / Serpentguard packs, kill Fathom-Witches first, then Coilfang Serpentguards. Serpentguard uses Spell Reflection and Corrupt Devotion Aura; Nether-Mage destruction buffs can be dispelled or spellstolen, and Greyheart Skulker kicks nearby casters.
D. Pre-Morogrim Trash
Local priority
  1. Tidewalker Depth-Seer.
  2. Tidewalker Hydromancer.
  3. Tidewalker Shaman.
  4. Tidewalker Warrior.
  5. Tidewalker Lurker / Harpooner / minor murlocs.
Depth-Seer first
Tidewalker Depth-Seer casts Healing Touch, which must be interrupted, and Rejuvenation on other murlocs. Keep it controlled until it dies.
Warrior control
Tidewalker Warrior Frenzy increases attack speed heavily and should be removed with Tranquilizing Shot. Bloodthirst deals damage and heals the mob, and Uppercut knocks back.
Hydromancer casts
Tidewalker Hydromancer uses Frost Nova, Frost Shock, and Frostbolt. Frostbolt can be interrupted to reduce tank and raid pressure.
Shaman spacing
Tidewalker Shaman Chain Lightning punishes stacked players, Lightning Bolt can be interrupted, and Lightning Shield damages attackers, especially auto attackers.
Interrupt healers and bolts
Assign kicks to Healing Touch first, then Frostbolt and Lightning Bolt. Kill priority casters before padding on clumped murlocs.
AoE after control
Tidewalker Lurker and Tidewalker Harpooner are mostly melee damage mobs with net / bleed style minor pressure. Minor murlocs are usually clumped with dangerous mobs and AoE’d once priority mobs are controlled.
E. When In Doubt Priority
When in doubt
  1. MC / knockback wipe mobs.
  2. Healers.
  3. Totems that create adds.
  4. Beast-Tamer / dangerous summons.
  5. Armor or aura mobs.
  6. Fear / cleave mobs.
  7. Casters.
  8. Minor AoE adds.
