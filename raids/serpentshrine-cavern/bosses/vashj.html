Phase 1 · 100% to 70% · tank and spank
Main tank on Vashj
Main tank holds Vashj on her platform. Standard tanking until 70%.
Shock Blast tank stun
Massive damage to main tank plus five second stun. Shaman in main tank group drops Grounding Totem to absorb. Off tank picks up if main tank drops too low.
Entangle on tank
Roots main tank in place. Paladin uses Blessing of Freedom; druid uses shapeshift. Do not let it stay up.
Static Charge spread out
Random player gets hit by Static Charge. Deals 2k Nature damage to anyone within 5 yards. If targeted, run away from raid. Healers stay 5+ yards apart at all times.
Save mana for Phase Three
Phase One healing is light. Conserve mana for the Phase 3 sporebat phase.
Pre-position for Phase Two
Know your pylon location. When Vashj hits 71%, start moving toward your assigned spot.
Stop at 71% to position for Phase Two
Do not push into 70% accidentally. Slow DPS at 73–72% and confirm everyone is at their Phase Two position before crossing.
Stay 5+ yards apart
Static Charge is the main raid damage in Phase One. Stay 5+ yards apart on the inner ring. Raid clusters near boss but maintains spread for charge splash.
Hold Bloodlust for Phase Three
Do not burn Bloodlust early. Save it for the enrage race in Phase 3.
Phase 2 · Vashj Damage Immune · Deactivate generators · Kill adds
Vashj shields, main tank free
Vashj becomes invulnerable in center. Main tank stays nearby. Threat resets when last pylon drops; main tank must be ready to grab her instantly.
Coilfang Elites one-shot cloth
Off tank grabs Elites instantly. Tank facing away from raid, melee assist.
Persuasion mind controls
Vashj mind controls random player and boosts their damage. Off tank taunts/picks up MCed player. Mind controlled player cannot be CC'd.
Watch the strider tank
Strider tank kites Striders in a loop on the south outer ring, away from raid. Dedicate a healer to follow them. If the strider tank dies, Striders walk into the raid and Panic-fear everyone.
Striders fear in melee
Coilfang Striders pulse AoE fear every 3s in melee range. Healers stay clear. All classes with DoTs throw them on Striders.
AoE healing required
Constant chip damage from elemental hits plus Forked Lightning cone. Spread heal duties. Do not tunnel any one tank.
Tainted Cores relay chain
Tainted Elementals drop cores. Looter is rooted on pickup. Target the next person in the relay chain and use the Tainted Core macro. Cores route to Pylon 1 (NW), Pylon 2 (NE), Pylon 3 (SE), Pylon 4 (SW).
Tainted Elementals 15s kill window
Tainted Elemental's Spawn every ~50s but despawn in 15–20s if not killed. The tainted killer assigned to that quadrant's pylon team burns it instantly and loots the core.
Enchanted Elementals must die before they reach Vashj
Each one that reaches her gives her a +5% boss damage stack. Ranged DPS picks them off the second they spawn at the water's edge.
Striders are never melee only dots
Strider tank kites along the south outer-ring loop. Ranged DoT every Strider as they pass. Warlocks shine here. Melee cannot touch the strider.
Drop all four pylons within ~10s
All four pylons must come down close together. Coordinate timing on voice. Adds must be controlled before final pylon drops.
Phase 3 · 50% to 0% · Phase 1 mechanics + Spore Bats spawning · Race the Enrage
Main tank grabs Vashj on threat reset
Last pylon dropping wipes threat. Main tank taunts immediately. Main tank misdirect should be ready.
All Phase 1 abilities back
Shock Blast, Entangle, Static Charge resume. Grounding Totem rotation continues. Blessing of Freedom and shapeshift breaks Entangle.
Ground poison accumulates
Spore Bats spit poison patches that linger on the ground. The longer Phase 3 lasts, the less safe ground remains. Heal smart and use saved mana for the burn.
Cooldowns
This is the burn phase. Use everything left Innervate chains, Mana Pot and Mana Tide. If you are still saving cooldowns, you saved them too long.
Spore Bats the ranged kill priority
Bats spawn slowly at first, then accelerate. Ranged DPS focuses bats above all else. Ground poison accumulates and the raid runs out of safe ground if too many drop.
Bloodlust window
Pop everything. Trinkets, racials, potions, Bloodlust. Race the clock.
Move out of poison patches
Do not stand in green ground poison. Main tank moves Vashj to clean ground when current spot fills up. Maintain 5+ yard spread for Static Charge.
Phase 1 positioning
Raid spreads 5+ yards for Static Charge while main tank holds Vashj on her platform.
Phase 2 · pylon positioning
Pylon 1 NW, Pylon 2 NE, Pylon 3 SE, Pylon 4 SW. Core relay routes to assigned pylon players. Avoid striders being kited. Melee DPS adds. Ranged kill elementals before they reach Vashj.
Phase 3 · sub-50% burn
Vashj returns with Phase 1 mechanics plus Spore Bats. Main tank moves Vashj to clean ground as poison spreads.
Wipe Causes
  • Push past 70% before everyone is in Phase 2 position.
  • Slow DPS on Tainted Elemental preventing the core drop, dragging phase 2 out.
  • Strider tank dies, Panic fear hits raid, wipe.
  • Melee touches a Strider and dies.
  • Enchanted Elemental reaches Vashj for +5% boss damage stack, main tank eventually dies.
  • Healing not spread far enough across the platform.
  • Slow DPS in Phase 3 causing ground poison patches multiply, platform becomes unhealable.
  • Main tank misses Vashj on threat reset after the last pylon, Vashj walks into the raid.
Tainted Core Macro
/use Tainted Core /y <------- TAINTED CORE TO: %t !!! /s <------- TAINTED CORE TO: %t !!! /script SendChatMessage("!!! YOU HAVE THE CORE !!!", "WHISPER", "Common", UnitName("target"));
Target the next person in the relay chain, then mash the macro. Yells, says, and whispers them so they cannot miss it.
